# Stages and Readings

## Pre-submission Requirements (art. 34 Regulaminu Sejmu)

Every project must include **uzasadnienie** containing:
- Need and purpose of the law
- Current state of the regulated area and difference vs. proposed state
- Expected social, economic, financial, and legal effects
- Funding sources (if budget impact)
- Assumptions for executive acts (rozporzadzenia)
- Statement of EU law compatibility (or that subject not covered by EU law)

**Rzadowy**: additionally **OSR** (Ocena Skutkow Regulacji) with consultation results - obligatory (Regulamin pracy RM para. 28 ust. 2). OSR covers: affected entities, consultation results, budget impact, competitiveness/SME/family/environment effects.

**Poselski** (from 31 Aug 2024): **DSR** (Deklarowane Skutki Regulacji) on a standard form introduced by Uchwala Prezydium Sejmu nr 51 z 26.08.2024.

**Obywatelski**: 1,000 signatures with committee notification, then 100,000 signatures within 3 months of Marszalek's ruling.

---

## I Czytanie (First Reading)

**Timing**: no earlier than **7 days** after doreczenie druku to MPs (art. 37 ust. 4).

**Where it takes place** - default on committee session; obligatorily on plenary session for:
- Constitutional amendment bills (zmiana Konstytucji)
- Budget laws and provisoria
- Tax laws (podatkowe)
- Electoral laws (Prezydent, Sejm, Senat, local government)
- Laws on structure/jurisdiction of public authorities
- Codes (kodeksy)
- Any project additionally directed there by Marszalek for "important reasons"

**Content**: wnioskodawca presents uzasadnienie, debate on general principles, MPs' questions.

**Can end with**: motion to reject project in whole (wniosek o odrzucenie projektu w calosci) - voted on if I czytanie held on plenum. If rejected -> terminal state ODRZUCONY.

**After**: Sejm resolution to refer to committee (one or several); committee begins detailed work.

---

## Committee Work (Between I and II Czytanie)

Committee can:
- Appoint a **podkomisja** (stala or nadzwyczajna) to work on details
- Request **opinions** from other committees
- Commission expert opinions (BAS/BEOS, academic experts)
- Hold **wysluchanie publiczne** (art. 70a-70i) - if committee votes for it after I czytanie, before detailed work; published at least 14 days ahead (3 days for urgent/budget projects)

### Obligatory opinions via BAS/BEOS:
- Compatibility with EU law (art. 34 ust. 9)
- Whether the project implements EU law (art. 95a ust. 3)

### Other consultation bodies (depending on subject matter):
- **Komisja Wspolna Rzadu i Samorzadu Terytorialnego** - mandatory when bill affects local government; 14-day response window
- SN, NSA, KRS, RPO, NIK, NBP, GUS, Prokurator Generalny
- NSZZ (trade unions), employer organizations
- EBC and EU institutions (when EU notification required)

**Rada Ministrow** issues a formal **stanowisko** for non-government projects.

### Sprawozdanie komisji (result - separate druk)

Contains one of three recommendations:
1. Accept **without amendments** (bez poprawek)
2. Accept **with amendments** (z poprawkami) - includes unified text (tekst jednolity)
3. **Reject** the project (odrzucenie)

Also contains **wnioski mniejszosci** - rejected amendments that min. **5 member-MPs** requested in writing. Each has its own justification showing difference from the main report.

Committee elects a **posel sprawozdawca** (rapporteur) who presents at II czytanie.

---

## II Czytanie (Second Reading)

**Always on plenary session** (posiedzenie plenarne Sejmu).

**Timing**: no earlier than **7 days** after doreczenie sprawozdania to MPs (unless Sejm decides otherwise).

**Content**: sprawozdawca presents sprawozdanie, debate, filing of poprawki and motions.

**Who can file poprawki** (art. 45 ust. 1):
- Wnioskodawca
- Komisja do Spraw Petycji
- Group of min. **15 poslow**
- Club/circle chair (or authorized vice-chair)
- Rada Ministrow

**Deadline to withdraw bill**: end of II czytanie (art. 119 ust. 4 Konstytucji). Last chance for wnioskodawca.

**If new amendments filed** -> project returns to committee for **dodatkowe sprawozdanie** (additional report - separate druk).

---

## III Czytanie (Third Reading)

Sprawozdawca presents dodatkowe sprawozdanie (if committee reconvened) or reads out filed amendments.

**Voting order** (art. 50 Regulaminu Sejmu):
1. Vote on **wniosek o odrzucenie projektu w calosci** (if filed)
2. Vote on each amendment (poprawka) **individually** - first those flagged as "rozstrzyga o innych" (determines other amendments)
3. Vote on the **project as a whole** in the final wording with accepted amendments

**Key constraint**: Marszalek Sejmu may refuse to put to a vote any amendment not previously submitted to committee (art. 119 ust. 3 Konstytucji).

After passage -> bill transmitted by Marszalek Sejmu to Marszalek Senatu and Prezydent.

---

## Points Where a Bill Can Be Terminated in Sejm

| Stage | Mechanism |
|---|---|
| Before I czytanie | Marszalek returns for formal deficiencies in uzasadnienie |
| Before I czytanie | Komisja Ustawodawcza opinion (3/5 majority): legally inadmissible |
| After I czytanie (plenum only) | Vote on wniosek o odrzucenie w calosci |
| In committee | Sprawozdanie recommends odrzucenie |
| II czytanie | Wnioskodawca withdraws (last opportunity) |
| III czytanie | Rejection vote passes, or final vote on whole project fails |
| After Senate / President | See references/senate-and-president.md |
| End of kadencja | Dyskontynuacja (below) |

---

## Dyskontynuacja (Discontinuation of Parliamentary Work)

**Rule**: End of Sejm term kills all pending work. New parliament starts fresh - materia must be re-filed. Constitutional principle derived from art. 98 Konstytucji (recognized by TK case law).

**Exceptions** (work that survives into the next kadencja):

| Exception | Source | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Obywatelski project** | art. 4 ust. 3 ustawy z 24.06.1999 | Survives into **one** following kadencja only. I czytanie must happen within 6 months of new Sejm's first session. If not passed then -> also expires. |
| **Komisja sledcza** reports not yet considered | - | |
| **Odpowiedzialnosc konstytucyjna** proceedings | before Trybunal Stanu | |
| **Komisja do Spraw UE** proceedings | treaty obligations | |
| **Petycje** | art. 126g Regulaminu Sejmu | One-time, for one kadencja only |

Practical example: a poselski project filed in kadencja VIII not passed before 11 Nov 2019 was permanently lost.
